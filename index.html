About Us

Wonder Kids

Wonder Kids is a cherished early learning establishment nestled in Sonari Gaon, Tarajan, Jorhat, Assam — a place where children take their very first steps into the world of knowledge and creativity. Located near the Naraya Petrol Pump on Brahmin Gaon Road, our school is easily accessible and deeply rooted in the local community, making it a trusted choice for families across the region.

At Wonder Kids, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a nurturing space to grow, explore, and discover. Our dedicated educators focus on holistic development — blending structured learning with play-based activities to build confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong love for learning. We welcome children Monday through Saturday during morning hours, ensuring a consistent and comforting routine for your little ones.

  • Conveniently located in Sonari Gaon, Jorhat — easily accessible for local families
  • Structured morning sessions from Monday to Saturday for a healthy routine
  • Child-friendly and safe environment focused on holistic early development
